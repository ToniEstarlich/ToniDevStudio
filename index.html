About Me / Sobre mí

Need a modern, responsive website or web app? I’m Antonio Estarlich, a junior full-stack web developer based in the UK. I design and build clean, mobile-first, accessible web solutions for individuals, startups, and small businesses.

My technical stack includes HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, Flask, Django, PostgreSQL, and version control with Git & GitHub. I develop both front-end user interfaces and back-end RESTful APIs with structured databases. I'm also familiar with Bootstrap, Jinja, JSON, and testing frameworks like JEST.

I hold diplomas in Web Development and Advanced Software Development, earned through Amazon UK's Career Choice programme over two years. I’m currently completing a Level 5 Diploma in Web Development at Bristol College, which is equivalent to the first two years of a UK university degree. My plan is to continue with a Level 6 or BSc in AI or Computer Science.

My portfolio includes real-world projects such as a dental clinic site, a dynamic news portal, an e-commerce store for digital art, and a Japanese learning app. These showcase my skills in authentication, CRUD operations, responsive design, database integration, and front-end interactivity.

I’m actively seeking a junior developer role where I can contribute to a collaborative team, grow professionally, and help deliver web solutions that make a real impact. I’m detail-oriented, reliable, and passionate about building useful and user-friendly applications.
